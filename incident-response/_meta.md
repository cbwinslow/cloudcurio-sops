# Incident Response and Emergency Procedures

## Scope and Domain

This directory contains incident response plans, emergency procedures, runbooks, and crisis management documentation for handling security incidents, system outages, and other critical events at CloudCurio.

## Purpose

The incident response documentation in this directory serves to:
- Define processes for detecting, responding to, and recovering from incidents
- Establish clear roles and responsibilities during incidents
- Minimize impact and recovery time for critical incidents
- Document post-incident review and improvement processes
- Ensure business continuity during emergencies
- Maintain stakeholder communication during crises

## Incident Severity Levels

- **P0/Critical**: Complete service outage or security breach with immediate business impact
- **P1/High**: Major functionality impaired or significant security risk
- **P2/Medium**: Partial service degradation with workaround available
- **P3/Low**: Minor issues with minimal user impact
- **P4/Informational**: Documentation, questions, or enhancement requests

## Access and Availability

Incident response procedures must be:
- Accessible 24/7 to authorized personnel
- Available offline in case of network failures
- Regularly tested and validated
- Updated based on lessons learned

## Target Audience

These procedures are intended for:
- Incident response team members
- On-call engineers
- System administrators
- Security team
- Management and executives
- Customer support teams
- Anyone who may need to respond to or be involved in incidents

## Testing and Drills

Incident response procedures should be:
- Tested through tabletop exercises quarterly
- Validated through fire drills semi-annually
- Updated after each actual incident
- Reviewed and improved based on testing outcomes

## Review Schedule

Incident response documentation must be reviewed:
- Quarterly for currency and accuracy
- After every major incident (post-incident review)
- When infrastructure or architecture changes
- When team composition or roles change
- Annually as part of business continuity planning
