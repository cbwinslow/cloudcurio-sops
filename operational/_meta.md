# Operational Procedures

## Scope and Domain

This directory contains day-to-day operational procedures, workflows, and standard operating procedures that guide routine business operations within CloudCurio.

## Purpose

The operational procedures documented here are designed to:
- Standardize common operational tasks and workflows
- Reduce operational errors through clear, documented processes
- Enable consistent service delivery across teams
- Facilitate knowledge transfer and cross-training
- Provide reference material for routine operations

## Content Areas

This directory includes procedures for:

### Daily Operations
- System monitoring and health checks
- Routine maintenance procedures
- Service level management
- Capacity planning and resource management

### Business Processes
- Customer onboarding and offboarding
- Support ticket handling and escalation
- Change management workflows
- Release and deployment coordination

### Communication
- Internal communication protocols
- Status update procedures
- Meeting standards and guidelines
- Documentation requirements

### Administrative
- Access request procedures
- Asset management
- Vendor management processes
- Budget and expense procedures

## Document Organization

Documents should be organized by functional area or process type. Each procedure document should include:
- Purpose and scope
- Prerequisites
- Step-by-step instructions
- Expected outcomes
- Troubleshooting guidance
- Owner and review date

## Target Audience

These procedures are intended for:
- Operations team members
- Support staff
- Team leads and managers
- Any personnel involved in day-to-day operations

## Review Schedule

Operational procedures should be reviewed quarterly or when significant process changes occur to ensure they remain current and accurate.
