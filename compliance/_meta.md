# Compliance and Regulatory Procedures

## Scope and Domain

This directory contains compliance requirements, regulatory documentation, audit procedures, and governance policies that ensure CloudCurio meets all applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

## Purpose

The compliance documentation in this directory serves to:
- Define regulatory compliance requirements and procedures
- Document audit preparation and response processes
- Maintain evidence of compliance for certifications
- Establish governance frameworks and controls
- Ensure data privacy and protection compliance
- Facilitate regulatory reporting and disclosures

## Content Areas

This directory includes documentation for:

### Regulatory Compliance
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) procedures
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) compliance
- HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) if applicable
- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act) controls if applicable
- Industry-specific regulations
- International compliance requirements

### Certifications and Standards
- ISO 27001 compliance procedures
- SOC 2 (System and Organization Controls) requirements
- PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) if applicable
- NIST framework alignment
- Other certification maintenance procedures

### Data Privacy and Protection
- Privacy policy implementation
- Data subject rights procedures (access, deletion, portability)
- Consent management processes
- Data processing agreements
- Privacy impact assessments
- Cross-border data transfer procedures

### Audit Management
- Internal audit schedules and procedures
- External audit preparation
- Audit evidence collection and maintenance
- Audit finding remediation processes
- Continuous compliance monitoring

### Risk Management
- Risk assessment procedures
- Risk register maintenance
- Control effectiveness testing
- Third-party risk management
- Risk mitigation strategies

### Governance and Policies
- Corporate governance structure
- Policy development and approval process
- Policy exception procedures
- Compliance training requirements
- Conflict of interest procedures

### Records and Documentation
- Record retention policies
- Document management procedures
- Evidence preservation
- Legal hold procedures
- Archiving and disposal processes

## Confidentiality

Compliance documentation may contain sensitive business information, audit findings, or legal matters. Access should be restricted to authorized personnel only.

## Target Audience

These documents are intended for:
- Compliance officers
- Legal team members
- Audit teams (internal and external)
- Executive management
- Privacy officers
- Risk management personnel
- Designated compliance coordinators

## Review and Maintenance

Compliance procedures must be reviewed:
- Annually at minimum
- When regulatory requirements change
- Following audit findings or compliance gaps
- When business operations significantly change
- After data breach or compliance incidents

All compliance documents must maintain version history and approval records.
