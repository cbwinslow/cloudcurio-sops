# Automation and Ansible Standards

## Domain

This directory contains standards for automation, configuration management, and Ansible playbook development.

## Contents

- **playbook-standards/** - Standards for writing and organizing Ansible playbooks
- **idempotency-policy/** - Policies ensuring automation tasks are idempotent
- **secret-injection-guidelines/** - Guidelines for securely injecting secrets into automation workflows
- **auto-remediation-workflows/** - Standards for automated remediation workflows
- **gitops-integration/** - Standards for integrating automation with GitOps practices

## Scope

Covers automation practices, Ansible usage, and configuration management to ensure reliable and secure automation.
