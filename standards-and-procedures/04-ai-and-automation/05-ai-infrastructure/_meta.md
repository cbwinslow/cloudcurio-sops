# AI Infrastructure Standards

## Domain

This directory contains infrastructure standards specific to AI/ML workloads, including GPU scheduling, containerization, and disaster recovery.

## Contents

- **gpu-scheduling-and-allocation/** - Standards for scheduling and allocating GPU resources for AI workloads
- **containerization-of-llms/** - Standards for containerizing large language models
- **telemetry-and-sandboxing/** - Telemetry and sandboxing requirements for AI workloads
- **privacy-preserving-ml/** - Standards for privacy-preserving machine learning techniques
- **disaster-recovery-for-ai/** - Disaster recovery procedures specific to AI/ML systems

## Scope

Addresses the unique infrastructure requirements of AI/ML workloads including compute, storage, and operational resilience.
