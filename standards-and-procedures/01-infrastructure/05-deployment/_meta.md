# Deployment Standards

## Domain

This directory contains standards for deploying services, applications, and infrastructure components, including base images, patching, and rollback procedures.

## Contents

- **base-image-standards/** - Standards for creating and maintaining base images
- **patch-management/** - Patch management policies and procedures
- **service-onboarding/** - Procedures for onboarding new services to infrastructure
- **container-hardening/** - Container security and hardening standards
- **rollback-checklist/** - Checklists and procedures for rolling back deployments

## Scope

Covers deployment processes, standards, and procedures to ensure reliable and secure service delivery.
