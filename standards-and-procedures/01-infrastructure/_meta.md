# Infrastructure Standards

## Domain

This directory contains standards and procedures for infrastructure management, including network, compute, storage, monitoring, and deployment operations.

## Contents

- **01-network/** - Network topology, routing, VPN, segmentation, and monitoring standards
- **02-compute/** - Compute resource standards including naming, virtualization, and resource allocation
- **03-storage/** - Storage management, backup, recovery, and encryption standards
- **04-monitoring/** - Monitoring, alerting, telemetry, and anomaly detection procedures
- **05-deployment/** - Deployment standards including base images, patching, and service onboarding

## Scope

These standards govern the technical foundation of CloudCurio's infrastructure, ensuring reliability, security, and operational efficiency across all infrastructure components.
