# Governance Standards

## Domain

This directory contains governance, compliance, and organizational management standards.

## Contents

- **audit-framework/** - Audit framework and procedures for organizational compliance
- **change-management/** - Change management processes and approval workflows
- **roles-and-approval-matrix/** - Organizational roles and approval authority matrix
- **compliance-baselines/** - Compliance baseline requirements for various regulations
- **ethics-and-conduct/** - Code of ethics and conduct for personnel
- **escalation-contact-list/** - Contact information for escalations and emergencies

## Scope

Covers organizational governance, compliance requirements, and management processes to ensure accountability and regulatory adherence.
